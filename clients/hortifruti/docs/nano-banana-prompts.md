# 🍌 Prompts para Mídia Visual (Nano Banana / Midjourney / DALL-E)

> Use estes prompts na IA geradora de imagens (como Nano Banana) para criar o banco de imagens exclusivo do Hortifruti para rodar as estratégias do `marketing-calendar.md`.

---

## 📸 1. O Conceito "Frescor Premium" (Uso Geral)

**Prompt (Inglês recomendado):**
> High-quality commercial photography of a vibrant local farmers market display, focusing on fresh organic fruits and vegetables. Crisp lighting, shallow depth of field, bright colors, dewy water droplets on leafy greens. Professional food photography, 8k resolution, photorealistic, warm sunlight.

**Uso:** Fundo para posts de "Bom dia", anúncios de reabastecimento.

## 🍹 2. Segunda Detox (Suco Verde)

**Prompt:**
> A beautiful, refreshing glass of bright green detox juice on a rustic wooden table. Next to the glass: fresh kale leaves, half a green apple, a slice of lemon, and a small piece of ginger. Morning sunlight streaming through a window, bright and airy aesthetic, clean eating concept, high resolution, hyper-realistic.

**Uso:** Segunda-feira, dicas de detox e recomeço.

## 🍓 3. Terça da Fruta (Oferta Relâmpago)

**Prompt (Exemplo prático: Manga):**
> Perfect, ripe, juicy Palmer mangoes stacked neatly in a rustic wooden crate. One mango is sliced open revealing perfectly vibrant orange, juicy flesh. Farmers market setting, warm and inviting atmosphere, macro photography, vivid colors, highly detailed, appetizing.

**Uso:** Posts carrossel com benefícios ou flash sales. *Troque "Palmer mangoes" pela fruta da promoção.*

## 🥗 4. Quarta Verde (Combos / Kit Salada)

**Prompt:**
> Top-down view of a fresh salad preparation. A rustic wooden cutting board surrounded by vibrant red cherry tomatoes, crisp green lettuce, vibrant purple onions, and a bottle of olive oil. Healthy cooking, raw ingredients, vibrant contrast, cinematic lighting, appetizing food photography.

**Uso:** Para vender o "Combo Salada" puxando pelo visual.

## 🧺 5. Cesta Cativa / Fim de Semana (Programa de Assinatura)

**Prompt:**
> A rustic woven basket overflowing with a colorful variety of fresh, premium organic produce. Inside the basket: bright red apples, bananas, a bunch of deep green spinach, bright orange carrots, and shiny tomatoes. Set on a wooden table, sunlight, wholesome, farm-to-table aesthetic, high definition.

**Uso:** Vender o formato de Cesta em Casa (Programa Semanal).

## 🥩 6. Sexta do Churrasco (Kit Vinagrete)

**Prompt:**
> Close-up professional photography of the perfect Brazilian vinaigrette ingredients (vinagrete): diced ripe red tomatoes, chopped onions, and fresh green parsley elegantly arranged on a dark slate board. A knife resting nearby. High contrast, sharp focus, vibrant and fresh, culinary magazine style.

**Uso:** O "O que não pode faltar no seu almoço de domingo" (Kit Vinagrete / Cebola / Alho).

---

### 💡 Dicas pro Nano Banana:
- Sempre adicione **"Photorealistic, 8k, professional food photography"** no final para garantir o aspecto real de comércio (e evitar o estilo desenho/cartoon).
- Para as artes com texto por cima, adicione **"negative space on the left"** ou **"negative space at the top"** (espaço vazio para você poder escrever o preço no Instagram).
